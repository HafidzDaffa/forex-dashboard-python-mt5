📖 Volume Delta Legend

Volume Delta (Δ)

Delta % = (Buy Vol − Sell Vol) / Total Vol × 100
Cumulative Delta = Running sum of deltas over time
Overall = Weighted avg (M5 heaviest → D1 lightest)
Net Δ = Total buy volume − total sell volume

Pressure Levels

▲ HEAVY BUYING Delta > +20%
